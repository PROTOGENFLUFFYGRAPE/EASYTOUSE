Discord Wick bot commands guide

basic commands

W!i (user id) — Shows information about the user.

W!cases — Shows most recent cases.

W!cases (user id) — Shows that user's cases.

W!warn (user id) ?r reason — Warns the user.

W!kick (user id) — Kicks the user.

W!timeout (user id) ?t time ?r reason — Timeouts the user.

How To Edit Warns

1. Use W!cases to find the case ID.

OR

Use W!cases (user id) to see that user's cases.


Edit using:

W!cases (case id) ?e ?r new reason

Timeout & Remove Timeout

W!timeout (user id) ?t TIME ?r reason

Ban & Unban

W!ban (user id) ?r reason — Bans the user.

W!unban (user id) ?r reason — Unbans the user.


Editing a ban:

You must unban then re-ban:

W!unban (user id) ?r reason

W!ban (user id) ?r new reason

Mass Kick / Ban / Timeout

W!kick USERID1 USERID2

W!ban USERID1 USERID2

W!timeout USERID1 USERID2 ?t TIME ?r reason
