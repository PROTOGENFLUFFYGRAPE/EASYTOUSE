Discord Wick Bot Guide

Reasons

Always include ?r reason when warning, banning, or timing out users.

Warns

W!warn (user id) ?r reason — Warn a user and log it.

Bans

W!ban (user id) ?r reason — Permanently ban a user.

W!hackban (user id) — Ban user not in server.

W!unban (user id) ?r reason — Remove a ban.

Kicks

W!kick (user id) — Kick a user from the server.

Timeouts

W!timeout (user id) ?t TIME ?r reason — Temporarily mute a user.

W!delttimeout (user id) — Remove a timeout.

HOW TO EDIT WARNS

Two ways:

  1. W!cases — gives most recent cases at the top
  2. W!case (user id) — gives that user's cases

Then use W!cases (case id) ?e ?r new reason here to edit.

HOW TO BAN AND UNBAN

W!ban (user id) ?r ban reason here

HOW TO EDIT A BAN

There is no way to edit a ban directly:

Unban then re-ban with new reason:

W!unban (user id) ?r reason for unban

W!ban (user id) ?r ban reason here

HOW TO MASS BAN, KICK, TIMEOUT

W!ban USERID1 USERID2
